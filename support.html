Common Issues

  • Make sure your .usdz models that you want to import are located in the documents folder on your device.
  • The volume view is sometimes placed behind the preview view and you may need to move windows around to access it.
  • If the app crashes, try restarting your Vision Pro device and reopening the app.
  • For feature requests or bug reports, email us with screenshots if possible.

We aim to respond within 48 hours.
